About

I’m a UI Developer with a healthy interest in design. I'm most aligned with my work when my responsibilities are similar to the “Front end designer” role described in the 18F Front End Guide. In other words, if you need a developer that understands UX principles, has respect for aesthetics, can write HTML, CSS/SCSS and presentational JavaScript (vanilla or React), and loves design systems, then I’m probably your guy.

I place a high value on learning and collaboration and enjoy meeting people with the same mindset. If you're interested in sharing something with me or just want to say hi, please feel free to email me at espinalv1@gmail.com.
